Why Choose Shredz?

1.Instructors with an excellent and illustrious career

SHREDZ only hire instructors with at least 5 years of experience. We also strive to provide better quality classes each year through continuous feedback and quarterly instructor evaluations.

2. Flexible and adjustable scheduling systems

Attendees are free to book and cancel classes at their convenience within the available time for each instructor.

3. A variety of classes

Shrez has offered not only the basic classes, but also reflects attendees' opinions and open classes that they want.
